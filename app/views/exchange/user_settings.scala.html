Fill up the data required above and press "Next". You will receive an email to confirm your registration. Follow email instructions to confirm and further complete the registration steps.

Step 2 - Personal Info

Fields above can be left empty for a while, but to comply to legal requirements, some of your operations (specially withdrawals) can only be executed if all fields above are correctly filled and documents verified.

Documents verification process is performed by Direct Pay team. When submission occurs in business hours, the verification usually takes less than an hour. Pictures must be clear and readable. If documents are not according to your registration, you will be notified at the registration form.

Step 3 - Financial Connections

The correct selection of bank, agency and account is needed to allow withdrawals. That account must be in the informed name and CPF. In any case, all operations are executed based on that data and you are responsible for providing the correct information (accurate and up to date).

The selection of Partner and Automatic options are features made available in order to simplify operations and make all the process easier and faster for you. This option is specially recommended if you always deals with one partner only.

If "Automatic Operations" is checked, any deposit made to your account will be (after verification) automatically converted to crypto-currency and sent to the selected partner. Also the crypto-ammount received from partner will be automatically converted to national currency and withdrawed to the informed bank account.

If you have regular operations with more than one of our Partners or if you do not want the operations to be automatically performed by Direct Pay, let unchecked the "Automatic Operations" checkbox.
